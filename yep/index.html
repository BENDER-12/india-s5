Taj Mahal,Agra
A symbol of love and one of the Seven Wonders of the World, the Taj Mahal is an architectural marvel built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. The intricate marble inlay work and the serene Yamuna River backdrop make this monument unforgettable.
Jaipur,Rajasthan-the pink city
Jaipur, known as the "Pink City," is a vibrant destination filled with palaces, forts, and colorful bazaars. Don't miss the stunning Amber Fort, the intricate Hawa Mahal, and the luxurious City Palace.
Kerala
From tranquil backwaters in Alleppey to lush tea gardens in Munnar, Kerala is a paradise for nature lovers. A houseboat cruise through the backwaters is a must for anyone seeking a relaxing escape.

Ladakh (Land of High Passes)
Known for its rugged terrain and crystal-clear skies, Ladakh is a dream destination for adventure seekers. From monasteries perched on hilltops to the breathtaking Pangong Lake, Ladakh's beauty is unparalleled.
